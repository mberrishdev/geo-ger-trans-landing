Services

I offer certified interpreting and translation services in Georgian–German and German–Georgian for both private and institutional clients across Germany. My services are fast, precise, and tailored to your legal, medical, administrative, and business needs.

Interpreting

Whether on-site or remote, I provide professional interpreting services for a wide range of situations:

  • Court Hearings & Legal Proceedings
  • Business Meetings & Negotiations
  • Government Appointments
  • Medical & Social Services
  • Conferences & Seminars
  • Remote Interpreting (via telephone, Skype, Zoom, etc.)
Types of Interpreting:
  • Consecutive Interpreting – Suitable for legal settings, official meetings, and consultations
  • Simultaneous Interpreting – For conferences, speeches, and multilingual events

I ensure clear and accurate communication between parties, while maintaining strict confidentiality and professional conduct.

Translation

I provide certified written translations for a wide variety of documents. Each translation includes my official stamp, a unique file number, and is archived for verification purposes.
Commonly Translated Documents:

  • Legal Documents (contracts, court decisions, powers of attorney)
  • Official Certificates (birth, marriage, divorce, death certificates)
  • Academic Records (diplomas, transcripts, reference letters)
  • Business & Financial Documents
  • Medical Reports & Diagnoses
  • Immigration & Asylum Papers

Every translation is proofread and quality-checked to ensure full accuracy and professional presentation.

Quality Assurance & Certification
  • Certified Translations – Legally recognized and accepted by German authorities
  • File Archiving – Each translation receives a file number for authenticity
  • Notarization Coordination – Upon request, I assist in notarizing translations
  • Strict Confidentiality – All materials are handled securely and ethically
